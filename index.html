I'm icyy and I'm

Yo, my name is icyybands, also known as icyy. I am a 22 year old artist from the South side of Chicago. When I watch a movie, I don’t watch the movie for the actors, the action, or even the drama. Instead, I analyze the cinematography of films & translate its components into my work. I immerse myself into my creations which allows me to make my clients look their best! Growing up, I’ve always had an eye for digital content and creation. I first discovered Adobe Creative Cloud which allowed my creativity to flow endlessly for myself & for my clients. In my work, I tell my story through music & multimedia. My goal is to encourage people not to worry about the things that they’re going through in their lives and to keep pushing forward. I believe that my art is like a medicine because it makes others feel better about themselves. The ultimate goal is to take this generation of music & videography to the next level!
